Most Popular Desserts and Flavors

Inspiration

It was a thursday evening when I was trying to unwind by watching another random episode of friends: "The one where Chandler doesn't like dogs". As appalling it was to find out Chandler doesn't like dogs, it was even more appalling to find out Ross doesn't like ice cream! I mean Ice cream is my go to favorite dessert and I always have a tub in my fridge! That's when I decided to look up data on what people's favorite desserts are.

Graph Features

This graph is a zoomable sunburst graph... which since we are talking about desserts, has a hole in the middle to look like a donut!

Graph Explanation

This visualization was made with d3 using information got from this handy website.

Conclusion

I guess Ross isn't alone in not having ice cream as a favorite dessert. But chocolate seems to be a popular flavor in all categories! Personally, I would love a big piece of chocolate cake with vanilla ice cream, topped with hot fudge! *yum*
