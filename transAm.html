Cross Country Bike Trip Dashboard

Inspiration

"Nothing compares to the simple pleasure of a bike ride.". That is what John F. Kennedy once said. Personally, I cannot comment on that since I am not an avid cyclist. But I know a friend who believes that and is currently in the middle of his first cross country bike trip. This dashboard follows the path he is taking.

Graph Features

This dashboard's main controller is the calendar. Clicking on the date on the calendar selects the route travelled on that day on the map and the shows the distance travelled on the right hand graph. The calendar also acts as a heatmap for each day to show the average temperature for the day. Similarly, the bars in the right plot are colored depending on the elevation of the starting location.

Graph Explanation

This dashboard gave me a chance to widely explore the features of Tableau. I could easily create three worksheets for each graph (calendar, map, bar plot of distance) and combine them all in a single dahboard and set the calendar as a controller. Not a single line of code involved from my side and everything was just a simple drag and drop.

Conclusion

Well, I guess the main conclusion I got from plotting this graph is Tableau is very easy to use once you get used to it! And also a real life proof to me that "the body achieves what the mind believes"!
